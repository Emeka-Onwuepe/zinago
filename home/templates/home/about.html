ABOUT US

Zinago is a law and human resources firm that provides unmatched professional services to establishments, employers, and employees. We lessen the burden on the employer by helping them maximize the efficiency of employees leading to higher productivity levels. We are a professional, enthusiastic and innovative team, dedicated to providing professional HR Consulting Services and evolving recruitment, training, and employee management solutions that help our customers become more profitable. Your most valuable resources are your employees.

Our legal services include:

  • Business Affair
  • Debt and Asset Recovery
  • Pensions, Employment and Labour Laws
  • Family and Matrimonial Law
  • Intellectual Property - Patents, Copyrights and Trademark Registration
  • Company Commercial - Formations, Regulatory and Corporate Governance including Company Secretarial Services
  • Real Estate - Purchase, Leasing and Management of Properties

Our HR objective is to add value to the organizations we operate, as we capture every aspect and specialism of the profession and look at the underpinning skills, behavior, and knowledge that are needed to be successful. Our HR Profession Map focuses on levels of competence. We try not to constrain the development of an HR professional by the job level at which they are operating.
